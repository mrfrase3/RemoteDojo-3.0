What is RemoteDojo?

CoderDojo WA is a network of coding clubs throughout Western Australia. It is a place that children aged between seven and seventeen can come and learn to code, whether they have prior experience or are completely new to coding. They are taught by different mentors who work on a volunteer basis.

Many of these issues stem from the fact that there is no system in place to enable remote or online learning. This is the area of the system that our project will tackle. RemoteDojo allows for many of the students on the waiting lists to participate in activities run by the mentors online. It also allows users that are unable to meet the mentors due to distance or physical disabilities to connect to and join the CoderDojo club.

Why A Demo System?
